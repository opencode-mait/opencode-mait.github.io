About Us

We are Free and Open Source Software users from New Delhi. This community originated from MAIT, New Delhi, and now spans across multiple colleges within the Delhi NCR region. We comprise of students and professionals who use linux and similar Free and Open Source software. We believe in endorsing the use as well as the development of Free/ Libre Open Source Software.
